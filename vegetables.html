The Health Benefits of Vegetables

Eating a variety of vegetables helps improve your health and well-being.

Broccoli

Broccoli is a cruciferous vegetable rich in vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants.

  • High in vitamin C and K
  • Supports immune function
  • Promotes heart health
  • Anti-inflammatory properties

Cucumber

Cucumber is hydrating and low in calories, ideal for weight loss and skin health.

  • Rich in water and electrolytes
  • Supports digestion
  • Great for hydration and skin
  • Low calorie snack

Tomato

Tomatoes are packed with lycopene, a powerful antioxidant beneficial for your heart.

  • Supports heart health
  • Rich in vitamin C and potassium
  • Reduces cancer risk
  • Good for eye and skin health

Onion

Onions are known for their anti-inflammatory and antibacterial properties.

  • Improves bone density
  • Regulates blood sugar
  • Fights bacteria
  • Rich in antioxidants

Bell Pepper

Colorful and crunchy, peppers are packed with vitamins and great for eye health.

  • High in vitamin A and C
  • Protects eyes and skin
  • Supports immunity
  • Rich in antioxidants

Carrot

Carrots are high in beta-carotene, which supports vision and immune health.

  • Great for eyesight
  • Supports skin health
  • Lowers cholesterol
  • Boosts immunity

Corn

Corn is a whole grain rich in fiber and antioxidants that promote digestive health.

  • Good source of fiber
  • Supports eye health
  • Rich in B vitamins
  • Energy boosting

Peas

Green peas are protein-rich and a good source of vitamins, fiber, and antioxidants.

  • Improves digestion
  • Stabilizes blood sugar
  • Supports weight control
  • High in plant protein
